Tips for Reducing Waste

Here are some tips for reducing waste in your community:

  • Bring your own reusable bags to the grocery store
  • Use a refillable water bottle instead of buying bottled water
  • Recycle paper, plastic, and glass
  • Compost food waste
  • Choose products with minimal packaging

Get Involved

There are many ways to get involved in local environmental initiatives:

  • Join a local environmental group
  • Participate in a community clean-up event
  • Volunteer at a local park or nature preserve
  • Attend a local environmental conference or workshop
